Maya Srikanth

I'm a senior undergraduate at Caltech studying computer science and business economics management. For the past few years, I've been working with Professor Anandkumar and Professor R. Michael Alvarez to develop NLP (natural language processing) techniques for the study of social media discussions and online abuse. My research interests include information retrieval, neural conversational agents, human-computer interaction, and reinforcement learning in the context of NLP.
